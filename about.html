Our Story

Caring for Colombo's pets since 2022.

Established in 2022

Golden Paws Pet Shop is a modern family-owned pet store located in Colombo, Sri Lanka. Founded with a passion for animal welfare, we specialize in premium pet food, accessories, toys, and grooming products. We believe every pet deserves the best care at affordable prices.

Our Mission

To provide pet owners with high-quality, safe, and effective products that enhance the lives of their animal companions.

Our Vision

To be Sri Lanka's leading pet care destination, known for our expertise, friendly service, and commitment to pet health.
